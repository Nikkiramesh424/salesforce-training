## What is CRM?

CRM means Customer Relationship Management.

CRM is used by companies to store customer details, manage sales, and communicate with customers properly.

It helps companies maintain customer data and improve business.

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:

* manage customers
* track sales
* improve communication
* organize business workflow

Salesforce is cloud-based and easy to access.

## Important Concepts Learned

### Account

Account represents a company or organization.

Example:
College, hospital, company.

### Contact

Contact represents a person related to the account.

Example:
Student, employee, patient.

### Opportunity

Opportunity represents a possible deal or business chance.

Example:
Admission process or product sale.

## Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

First a person becomes a lead.
Then their details are stored as a contact.
If there is a possible business deal, it becomes an opportunity.
Finally they become a customer.
